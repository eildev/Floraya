Our Story

Crafted in Thailand, Loved Worldwide

Floyara brings you the essence of Thai skincare innovation for radiant, healthy skin.

At Floyara, we believe in the power of nature to unlock your skin’s true beauty. Inspired by Thailand’s rich botanical heritage, our products blend premium natural ingredients like Aloe Vera, Niacinamide, and Dragon Blood Extract to brighten, hydrate, and protect. Designed for all skin types, especially sensitive skin, our Night Body Cream, Perfect Facial Cream, and Daily Body Cream with SPF50+ deliver visible results without compromise. Join us on a journey to glowing, youthful skin with Floyara.
